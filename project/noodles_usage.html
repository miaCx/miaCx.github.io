Noodles can be used in a variety of different ways. Most people eat them; however, they can be used for other activities as well. Some people make intricate pasta towers out of noodles. Others create sculptures out of them. You can also use pasta as a writing utensil.

Astronaughts eat specially packaged food while in space that is meant to last for a long time. Momofuku Andou, founder of Nissin Food Products Co., Ltd. and inventor of cup noodles, created "Space Ram", or ramen made for consumption in space. Now, astronaghts can enjoy noodles even when they're not on Earth.
