Noodles are believed to have originated in China. According to National Geographic, the earliest record of noodles appears in a book written during China's Han Dynasty, sometime between 25 and 220 AD.

Pasta, a kind of noodle, is known to be invented in Italy. It is debated whether noodles were first made in China or in Italy, but for now, no one knows for sure.
