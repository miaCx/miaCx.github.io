With so many different varieties of noodles, there are a few exceptional dishes. Listed here are are a few you should try:

Truffled Bacon Mac & Cheese

The truffled bacon macaroni is made with bacon bits and truffles, with a parmesan and herb mix sprinkled on top. You can find this dish at Roy's locations across the United States, or in California at Anaheim, La Jolla, Newport Beach, Pasadena, Rancho Mirage, San Diego, Waterfront, San Francisco, and Woodland Hills.
