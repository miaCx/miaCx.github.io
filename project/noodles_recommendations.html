With so many different varieties of noodles, there are a few exceptional dishes. Listed here are are a few you should try:

Truffled Bacon Mac & Cheese

The truffled bacon macaroni is made with bacon bits and truffles, with a parmesan and herb mix sprinkled on top. You can find this dish at Roy's locations across the United States. California locations are:

  • Anaheim
  • La Jolla
  • Newport Beach
  • Pasadena
  • Rancho Mirage
  • San Diego
  • Waterfront
  • San Fransisco
  • Woodland Hills

Nongshim Instant Ramyun

Nongshim sets the bar for the best ramen. Whether you wish to put the spicy flavoring in or not is your choice. These noodles are great prepared in chicken broth with a couple of eggs dropped in.

Wokou Ramen's Drunken Noodles

The soft udon noodles are flavored with special soy sauce. The dish comes with beef, chicken, bacon, and a mix of vegetables. So far, the world has been blessed with only one Wokou Ramen venue, located in the Village at Pacific Highlands Ranch.
